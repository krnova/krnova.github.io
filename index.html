Proven Trading Strategies

EMA Crossover Strategy

74% Win Rate 5-15 min expiry

This strategy uses two exponential moving averages (EMA) to identify the prevailing trend and potential entry points. The 5-period EMA crossing above the 10-period EMA signals a CALL opportunity, while the opposite crossover suggests a PUT trade.

Implementation Steps:

  1. Apply 5-period and 10-period EMAs to your chart
  2. Wait for price to be clearly above both EMAs for CALL or below for PUT
  3. Enter trade when faster EMA crosses slower EMA in direction of trend
  4. Set expiry time to 5-15 minutes depending on timeframe
  5. Use 60-70% of your usual stake size for this strategy

MACD Trend Confirmation

68% Win Rate 15-30 min expiry

Combine MACD indicator with price action to confirm strong trends. This strategy works best on 15-minute charts during active trading sessions (London/New York overlap).

RSI Divergence Strategy

65% Win Rate 5-10 min expiry

Identify potential trend reversals using RSI divergence patterns. This advanced strategy requires patience but offers high reward potential when executed correctly.
